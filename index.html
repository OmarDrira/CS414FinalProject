Data Overview

The project utilized a dataset containing daily XRP/USD prices spanning from 2017 to 2023. The data was collected from the XRP USD (XRP-USD) price history and historical data provided by Yahoo! Finance. The dataset included various features such as Date, Open, High, Low, Close, and Volume. To ensure robust model training and evaluation, the data was divided into three sets: training, validation, and testing. Exploratory data analysis techniques, including line plots, were employed to gain a better understanding of the price trends within each set. Additionally, feature selection and engineering were performed by using a window of prior closing prices (5 days) as input to the LSTM model. Normalization techniques such as zero-base normalization or min-max normalization were applied to enhance training efficiency. Overall, the dataset provided the necessary historical price information for training and evaluating the predictive models.
